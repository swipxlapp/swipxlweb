Privacy Policy

Effective date: 9 June 2026  ·  Last updated: 9 June 2026

The short version: Swipxl analyzes your photos entirely on your device to help you delete clutter. Your photos are never uploaded, collected, or shared. We use anonymous analytics to understand general app usage, and your purchase records are handled by your app store to manage your subscription. We don't require an account and we don't sell your data.

This Privacy Policy explains how Swipxl ("Swipxl", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the Swipxl mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to this Policy.

1. Your Photos & Media — Stay on Your Device

Swipxl needs access to the photos on your device so you can review, organize, and delete them. This is the core function of the App.

  • All analysis is on-device. Detecting duplicates, blurry images, screenshots, similar/burst shots, dark photos, memes, and old photos happens locally on your phone using on-device processing (such as perceptual hashing and image metadata).
  • We never upload your photos. Your images and their contents are not transmitted to us or to any third party, and are not stored on any server.
  • We do not collect, sell, or share your photos or any data derived from their visual content.
  • Deletion is handled by your operating system. When you confirm a deletion, photos are moved to your device's Trash / Recently Deleted via the standard system dialog, so you can recover them within your gallery's retention window.

2. Information We Do Collect

Swipxl collects a limited amount of non-photo information to operate, improve, and support in-app purchases:

DataWhyIdentifies you?
Anonymous usage & app-interaction data (e.g., app opens, sessions, feature use, crashes/diagnostics) To understand general usage and improve the App No — not linked to your identity
Device & app identifiers (e.g., app-instance ID, device/OS model, app version) Required by analytics to count devices and group sessions No — pseudonymous, not used to track you across other apps
Purchase records (which product you bought, subscription/entitlement status) To unlock Pro features and let you restore purchases No name/email collected; tied to a pseudonymous app-user ID

We do not collect your name, email address, contacts, precise location, or account credentials, because Swipxl has no sign-up or login.

3. Third-Party Services

We rely on a small number of reputable providers to deliver these functions. They process data under their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase Analytics — anonymous, aggregated usage analytics and diagnostics. (Firebase privacy · Google Privacy Policy)
  • App store billing (Google Play / Apple App Store) — securely processes all in-app purchases and subscriptions. We never see or store your payment-card details.
  • RevenueCat — manages your subscription entitlement and purchase restoration on our behalf using a pseudonymous identifier. (RevenueCat privacy)

4. Permissions We Request

  • Photos and media (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES on Android / Photo Library access on iOS) — to display and let you manage and delete your photos. Without it, the App cannot function.
  • Notifications (optional) — to send occasional, on-device reminders to tidy your library. These are local notifications; you can turn them off anytime in the App or your system settings.

5. How We Use Information

  • To provide and operate the App's core photo-cleanup features.
  • To maintain and unlock your Pro entitlement and process/restore purchases.
  • To understand aggregate usage and fix bugs, so we can improve the App.

We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not sell or rent your information to anyone.

6. Data Retention

Your photos and cleanup history (such as deletion records and saved-photo lists) are stored locally on your device and are removed when you delete them in the App or uninstall the App. Anonymous analytics and purchase records are retained by our service providers per their retention policies.

7. Security

Data transmitted by our analytics and billing providers is encrypted in transit. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but because Swipxl keeps your photos on your device and collects no account information, your most sensitive data never leaves your phone.

8. Children's Privacy

Swipxl is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country) and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will address it.

9. Your Rights

Depending on where you live (for example, under the GDPR or CCPA), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data, or to object to certain processing. Because Swipxl collects no account data and keeps your photos on-device, most of your data is already under your direct control — uninstalling the App removes the on-device data. For analytics or purchase records held by our providers, contact us and we will help where we can. We do not sell personal information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will revise the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes, provide notice within the App or on this page. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance.

Questions about this Policy or your data? Email support@swipxl.app.
