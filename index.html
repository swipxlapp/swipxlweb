How it works

Three steps to a clean camera roll.

1

Scan

Swipxl scans your photo library entirely on-device, then surfaces the clutter on a dashboard with a storage ring that shows how much space each category is eating.

2

Swipe

Open a deck and swipe — left to delete, right to keep, tap the middle to zoom in. Changed your mind? Undo any swipe with a smooth swoop-back.

3

Clean

Confirm, and deleted photos move to your device's Trash / Recently Deleted — recoverable for ~30 days. Nothing is gone for good until you say so.
